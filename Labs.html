Computer Labs / Classroom


At Wallace Community College Selma, computers in the lab and in the classroom give students access to the Internet, databases with millions of articles and e-books, the library catalog, and the comprehensive Library website, all of which may be used to discover the information they need. This college has made it a priority to offer the greatest technology to all stakeholders, therefore all administrative and instructional tasks are now completed utilizing computers. Both in the library and the computer lab, students have free access to them.
